Academic Programs

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Our O-Level program offers a comprehensive and rich curriculum with sciences, humanities, and vocational subjects to prepare students for both academic and practical life. Kiswahili is compulsory from S1-S4.

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

The A-Level program offers specialization in Sciences, Arts, and Humanities with excellent teaching staff and modern facilities to ensure student success.
